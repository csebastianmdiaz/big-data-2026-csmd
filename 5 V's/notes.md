The five key characteristics used to define Big Data:

* **Volume:** The vast amount of data generated every second
* **Velocity:** The speed at which data is generated, moved, and processed
* **Variety:** The different types of data (structured, semi-structured, and unstructured)
* **Veracity:** The trustworthiness, quality, and accuracy of the data
* **Value:** The ability to turn data into useful insights and business value
